About TESSERA GRC

A consortium built on
measurement, not opinion.

TESSERA GRC is the global research arm of Scala Carta Foundation, a registered foundation headquartered in the United States. Our mission is to standardize the measurement of medication adherence across all care settings using the MAP (Multidimensional Adherence Parameters) instrument and the ATLAS platform. Learn about MAP and PEACS →

Where most adherence research relies on self-report bias or proxy endpoints, TESSERA members collect structured MAP data through a clinician-administered protocol that takes under three minutes at the point of care. That data feeds a normative global database, enabling cross-country benchmarking that has never been possible before.

Membership is tiered to serve every stakeholder: universities running validation studies, clinics measuring patient populations, individual researchers building their evidence base, graduate students earning their first publication credit, and industry partners seeking real-world adherence data for regulatory submissions.

Validated Measurement
All TESSERA studies use the MAP instrument, ensuring cross-site comparability. No bespoke questionnaires, no drift from the normative dataset.
Global Normative Database
Every consented assessment contributes to the TESSERA Normative Dataset: country-level, disease-category, and demographic PE score distributions.
Non-Profit Governance
Scala Carta Foundation governs TESSERA with a scientific advisory board. No commercial agenda drives study design. Members co-author, not co-market.

Process · How It Works

Three steps to
join the consortium.

From inquiry to active membership in under two weeks for most tiers. No complex procurement process. No lengthy onboarding forms.

1
Complete the Application

Fill out the membership application below with your institution, country, research focus, and preferred tier. Applications are reviewed within 5 business days.

2
Receive Your Workspace Key

After a brief alignment call, Scala Carta Foundation issues your ATLAS workspace key provisioned for your tier and data residency region. No installation required.

3
Activate and Contribute

Your TESSERA Study ID is issued, your team completes MAP certification, and your first assessments begin contributing to the global normative dataset.
